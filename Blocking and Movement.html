Blocking and Movement


Blocking

When you're attacked and you can't attack you have to do Perfect Block. To do it you need to touch with middle dot on image below the enemy's saber when it attacks you.

When you block and have stamina you can't be bodyhitted.


Movement

To lose less stamina in fight you can't be attacked in the side so move camera to the enemy. If you will be attacked in back you will be backhitted and killed.

You also have to how it said in here walk with shift in fight to regain stamina.
